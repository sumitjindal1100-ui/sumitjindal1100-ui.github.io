The Story

From one chair to a Halifax institution.

Founded in 2022 by Munish “Lalaji” Nagpal — winner of the Crown of Clippers Nova Scotia — Lala Ji grew from a single barber chair into Nova Scotia's fastest-growing men's grooming brand, and then beyond grooming entirely: a cozy downtown cafe and a full-service salon & spa.
